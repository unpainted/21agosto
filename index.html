Stray Kids' "ATE" Becomes Their 1st Album To Spend 4 Weeks In Top 22 Of Billboard 200

Stray Kids has now spent a full month in the top 22 of the Billboard 200 with their latest mini album “ATE”!

Last month, “ATE” debuted at No. 1 on Billboard’s Top 200 Albums chart, making Stray Kids the first group in Billboard 200 history to have their first five charting albums enter the chart at No. 1.

On August 20 local time, Billboard revealed that “ATE” was now spending its fourth consecutive week on the Billboard 200 at No. 22, marking the highest ranking achieved by a Stray Kids album in its fourth week on the chart.

“ATE” is now Stray Kids’ first album to spend four weeks in row in the top 22 of the Billboard 200. (While the group’s 2023 album “★★★★★ (5-STAR)” also spent four weeks in the top 22 of the chart last year, they were non-consecutive.)
